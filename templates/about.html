About

The basic idea we had was to implement AI and Deep learning in a real-world scenario. As of today, crime rates have been increasing due to the floods in Pakistan but the security and investigation methods remain the same as the traditional methods on going since the beginning of these departments. In the previous studies the detection of weapons and other illegal substances was detectable with some flaws. The previous versions of this AI were slower with the issue of occupying more frames to process the objects than an average human needs seconds to detect a threat around him. So, in our project we are trying to reduce that time and frame requirement to detect a threat or illegal activity. This will be achieved with the help of AI and deep learning. Objective of this project is to help in weapons, illegal activity or substance detection. The goal in the end we are trying to achieve in this project is to decrease the amount of crimes occurring in an area. We are trying to help the security and investigation departments to make decrease the fear of an average person traveling, working or doing an everyday activity. This project will increase the security level of an area drastically, so that no one can reduce the quality of life of a regular person trying to live a simple life.
